My Favorite Recipe: Crêpes in under 15 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Whisk flour, eggs, milk, butter, and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour a thin layer of batter and then swirl to coat the pan and form a circle.
  4. Cook for about 1 minute, then flip the crêpe and cook the other side for another minute.
  5. Serve with your favorite toppings.
